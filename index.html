About Us

To achieve economic freedom and to succeed, one has to make sacrifices and endure trying circumstances to attain their goal.
The ABANQOBI TOTAL SERVICES SOLUTIONS was founded on this premise: The willingness to endure and overcome trials in order to achieve economic freedom and financial liberation.

ABANQOBI TSS serve to be an active testament that any young mind in south Africa which is willing to put the time and make the necessary sacrifices can achieve greatness.

We aim to play a significant role in the global technological revolution by improving processes, operations, communications and learning methods to name a few. We’re committed to making a notable contribution within the country for the betterment of lives and communities through a fresh and strategic implementation of science and technology to everyday life.

Meet The Founders

Sibusiso Ntombela - CEO & Co-founder

Sibusiso boasts 10 years of financial and management experience in the Finance Industry , with a wealth of Taxation expertise. After completing his BCom Accounting Degree at the University of Johannesburg , He attained a Post Graduate Diploma in Busines Administration at Wits Business School and holds a Senior Management Development Programme from Rhodes University in collaboration with Duke Co-operate Education.

He’s gained a brilliant reputation for achieving excellence in varied operational environments through effective process management and streamlining.

Sibusiso’s well-developed managerial and motivational skills have enabled him to effortlessly handle and oversee diverse staffing resources.

 Sibusiso is currently doing his final year in a Postgraduate Diploma in Tax through UNISA.

Brian Mokone - COO & Co-founder

Brian is a Certified Microsoft Professional with a UNISA Diploma in Information Technology. He is currently studying for a BCom Degree in Informatics and IT Management. Brian has over 12 years of experience as an Engineer specializing
in corporate Information Technology and Infrastructure Support.

Having worked for CIT Business Solutions, Wesbank, StorTech and Standard bank, he gained significant experience in IT within the Banking Sector.
He’s focused mainly on Information Systems Environment which encompasses: Software development, system/business analysis and technical support consulting.

Brian’s vast experience in IT Service Management is coupled with a rich skillset to execute complex technical support for both hardware and software.
